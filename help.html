Passenger Help Guide

How to Use the Fare Calculator
  • Select your pickup station.
  • Select your dropoff station.
  • Check the discount box if you are a Student, Senior, or PWD.
  • Click "Compute Fare".
Train Safety Reminders
  • Stand behind the yellow line while waiting.
  • Give priority seats to seniors and PWDs.
  • Do not block train doors.
  • Always keep your belongings secure.
